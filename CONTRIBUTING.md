# Contributing

Contributions are welcome, especially improvements to radiology reasoning structure, safety language, evidence retrieval, templates, and de-identified educational examples.

## Do not submit patient data

Do not open issues or pull requests containing:

- Patient identifiers
- DICOM files
- Accession numbers or medical record numbers
- Exact service dates
- Screenshots with names, IDs, birth dates, barcodes, QR codes, or institution-specific identifiers
- Images that are not properly de-identified and permitted for educational sharing

## Good contributions

- Better structured reporting templates
- Additional body-region checklists
- Differential diagnosis pitfalls
- Guideline/reporting-system references
- De-identified correction-learning examples
- Wording that improves medical safety and avoids overclaiming

## Evidence standard

When adding disease-specific content, prefer:

1. Guidelines and official reporting systems
2. Major textbooks or well-known educational references
3. High-quality review articles
4. Multicenter studies or case series
5. Case reports only for rare or visually distinctive findings

Include DOI, PMID, or stable URLs when possible.
