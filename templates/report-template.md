# Report Drafting Template

```text
Findings:
[Describe relevant positive findings first, then important negatives.]

Impression:
1. [Most important diagnosis or abnormality.]
2. [Complication, staging, or urgent finding if present.]
3. [Recommended correlation/follow-up if appropriate.]
```

Use cautious language when data are incomplete:
- "Findings are suspicious for ..."
- "Consider ... in the appropriate clinical context."
- "Evaluation is limited by ..."
- "Recommend correlation with ..."
- "If clinically indicated, consider ..."
