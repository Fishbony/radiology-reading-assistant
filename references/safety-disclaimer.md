# Medical Safety Disclaimer

This project is intended for education, structured reasoning, and radiology workflow support only.

It is not a medical device, diagnostic software, triage system, or replacement for a qualified radiologist or licensed clinician. Outputs generated using this framework should not be used as the sole basis for diagnosis, treatment, patient triage, or clinical decision-making.

Radiology interpretation requires:
- Complete original imaging data, preferably DICOM
- Appropriate clinical history and physical examination findings
- Laboratory and pathology correlation when relevant
- Prior imaging comparison
- Knowledge of local protocols and scanner parameters
- Professional medical judgment

Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with local laws, institutional policies, patient privacy rules, and professional standards.

Do not upload or publish patient-identifying images or protected health information unless properly de-identified and legally permitted.
